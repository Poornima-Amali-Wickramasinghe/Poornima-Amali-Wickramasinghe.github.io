Hi, I'm Poornima Wickramasinghe. Welcome to my website!

I am an Astrophysics enthusiast. 🌌🔭💻

I am currently undertaking my Erasmus traineeship at Nicolaus Copernicus University in Toruń, working with the Massive Star Group.

My work focuses on using the Method of Interpolation for Single Star Evolution (METISSE) stellar evolution tool to model the evolution of both single and binary star systems and to analyse the properties and outcomes of the resulting systems. METISSE is an open-source stellar evolution framework that provides a fast and flexible alternative to traditional rapid stellar evolution prescriptions. By interpolating between pre-computed stellar evolution tracks, the tool can determine key stellar parameters, including luminosity, radius, and core mass, across different stages of stellar evolution.
